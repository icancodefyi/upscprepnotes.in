# PRESS RELEASE — For Immediate Release

## UPSCPrepNotes Launches India's Largest Verified Topper Marks Database with 280+ Profiles

**NEW DELHI, India — June 25, 2026** — UPSCPrepNotes, an intelligence platform for UPSC civil services exam preparation, today announced the launch of the country's most comprehensive verified topper marks database, featuring detailed score breakdowns for over 280 successful candidates from the 2022-2025 examination cycles.

The platform aggregates verified marks data from official UPSC gazette notifications and topper interviews, making it the first freely accessible resource of its kind at this scale. Each profile includes paper-wise marks across all General Studies papers, Essay, Optional subjects, and the interview stage.

Key features of the database:

- **280+ verified topper profiles** with full marks breakdowns
- **Paper-by-paper analysis** covering GS 1-4, Essay, Optional, and Interview
- **Per-topper preparation strategy** including study plans, booklists, coaching details, and subject-wise approach
- **Year-over-year trends** across 2022, 2023, 2024, and 2025 results
- **Free access** with no registration required for marks data
- **Answer copy compilations** available for 50+ toppers

"Until now, aspirants had to manually search through dozens of interviews and gazette notifications to piece together a single topper's marks," said Zaid Rakhange, founder of UPSCPrepNotes. "We've done that work and structured it into a searchable, filterable database. If a UPSC aspirant wants to know exactly what marks it takes to get AIR 1 in Sociology optional, or how much a 130+ scorer in GS1 typically scores in other papers, they can find that in seconds."

The platform also features an AI-powered tutor that answers UPSC-specific questions using vector search over the topper strategy database, and offers 2,700+ free study materials from top coaching institutes.
